📄 MBA Thesis
Agent Banking: A New Growth Sector for the Banking Industry
🎓 IBA, University of Dhaka 📅 2020 📊 Econometric Analysis
This thesis examines the agent banking landscape in Bangladesh through an econometric lens, analysing the performance of the top 6 agent banking operators. The research explores business models, outreach strategies, and sustainability metrics to identify what separates high-performing agent banking programs from the rest.
Core Finding
BRAC Bank's unique "lending-first" model was identified as a key driver for sustainable financial inclusion — contrasting sharply with the deposit-only focus of competitors. This model created a self-sustaining ecosystem that drove both financial access and profitability.

Improved Self-Pruning for Broadcasting in Ad Hoc Wireless Networks
🎓 AUST, Dhaka 📅 2017 🛜 Wireless Networks · Algorithms
Proposes the ISB (Improved Self-Pruning Based Broadcasting) algorithm — a reactive broadcasting technique that uses extended three-hop neighbour knowledge to dramatically reduce the number of forwarding nodes while keeping packet headers minimal. Simulation results show ISB outperforms both self-pruning and dominant pruning across all tested network densities.
Key Result
ISB reduced redundant rebroadcasts by up to 40% compared to classic self-pruning, while maintaining near-100% delivery coverage — demonstrating that three-hop knowledge is sufficient for near-optimal broadcast coverage in ad-hoc networks.
